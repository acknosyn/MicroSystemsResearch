SatTrack

The SatTrack is a solar powered GSM (Global System for Mobile Communications) GPS (Global Positioning System) tracker. The SatTrack uploads its log file to an FTP server once every 24 hours, giving you data when you need it. Which means you don't have to wait months to collect the unit to download the data or risk losing the data all together if the unit is lost.

Advanced energy harvesting circuitry provides a theoretical indefinite life time. SatTrack's weight is interchangeable. Without the outer case, the SatTrack weighs just 20 grams! With the protective case, the unit still only weighs a total of 25 grams.

SatTrack is mainly used for tracking animals like medium to large birds or other animals. However, SatTrack can be customised to anyone's needs; we will personally develop a solution for your requirements.
