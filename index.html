About Us

We are a progressive company always striving to do the impossible. Through our founder and CEO Gian F. Badraun we have a lifetime of experience in the fields of micro electronic design and manufacture as well as unmanned aviation and remote telemetry. We design and build custom made equipment utilizing manual SMD assembly techniques. Our main focus is being involved in wild life spatial research by manufacturing a variety of GPS based animal tracking devices ranging from 3.5 g GPS loggers (that need to be recovered to obtain the position data) to 25 g GSM Solar powered GPS trackers.

What Do We Do?

 

Research

Our research includes developing a semi-autonomous pest control system called RATS (Rodent Autonomous Terminator System). This involves a drone autonomously dropping a quantity of approved rodent poison at the targeted GPS coordinates of rodent activity.

SatTrack

The SatTrack is a solar powered GPS (Global Positioning System) tracker. The SatTrack uploads its log file to an FTP server once every 24 hours, giving you data when you need it.
